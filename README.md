***Eco Class
Ecoclass turns forgotten school supplies into learning opportunities, connecting donors with students in need. This project solves the issue of resource scarcity and promotes solidarity, ensuring no student is left behind for not having basic school materials.

*About the Project
The project is being developed as part of the curriculum for the technical course in Systems Analysis and Development at the Senac Erechim institution. Its content consists of creating a collaborative digital platform focused on the donation and reception of school supplies, with the aim of facilitating access to education and reducing inequalities in the learning process.
The initiative aims to connect people who want to donate materials in good condition—such as notebooks, pencils, backpacks, textbooks, and more—with students and families facing financial difficulties in acquiring them. Through a simple and accessible system, users can register items for donation, view available materials, and request the ones they need.
The project also includes a points system, where citizens who make donations earn a score according to established criteria based on the type and quality of the materials. Subsequently, these points can be exchanged for discounts on products from partner stores, thereby encouraging and rewarding those who choose to do a good deed. 

**Features
Check out the main features below:
1.I Want to Donate! - Registering Materials: This allows the inclusion of materials for donation, including a photo, category, name, description, condition, and drop-off point.
2.I Need to Receive - Material Search: Students who need materials can view and request items, filling out a request form that directly notifies the school they attend.
3.User Registration: Individual users (students and non-students) can register as a person. Educational institutions and companies register as a legal entity.
4.Request - Requesting Materials: Users can request the materials they need.
5.More Ways to Do Good - Promoting Campaigns: This enables the promotion of donation drives, increasing visibility for the cause.
6.Project Partners - Sponsoring Companies: This feature showcases the project's sponsoring companies, showing where points can be redeemed for discounts.
7.Points System: Donors receive points based on the materials they donate, which can be exchanged for discounts or coupons at the project's partner companies. These points are determined by main categories based on the table below, depending on the item's quality at the time of registration.
8.Where to Donate - Drop-off Locations: This shows which schools are available to pick up and drop off materials.
